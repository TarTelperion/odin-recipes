Mochi

Ingredients:

  • Rice Flour
  • Milk
  • Red Bean Paste
  • Ice Cream

Steps:

  1. Combine Ingredients in the appropriate manner, and in the correct order.
  2. Microwave ingredients for the right amount of time. Stir occasionally if you feel like that's the right thing to do.
  3. Wrap the Mochi filling in the correct way with the Mochi dough, don't make any mistakes.
