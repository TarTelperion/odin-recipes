Just Bread

Description:

Literally just bread, don't overthink it.

Ingredients

  • bread
  • bread powder
  • bread sauce
  • bread crust

Procedure:

  1. Put bread crust on the bread
  2. Knead, and sprinkle with bread powder.
  3. Bake at 1 degree kelvin for twenty minutes.
  4. Serve warm with bread sauce.
